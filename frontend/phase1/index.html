Welcome to the Candidate Portal

The Entrance Commissionerate, established by the State Government, is the statutory body responsible for conducting entrance examinations and managing centralized allotment for admission to professional courses in Kerala. This portal provides candidates with a single-window system for registration, exam management, allotment tracking, and document verification across all major professional streams including Engineering, Medical, Legal, and Paramedical courses. The Centralized Allotment Process (CAP) ensures transparent, merit-based admissions across all participating institutions.
